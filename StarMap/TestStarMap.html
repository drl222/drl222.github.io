Star Map (Plate Carré/Orthographic)

In Plate Carré mode, click and drag left or right to scroll through the constellations. Right click to zoom into a particular location, in Orthographic mode.

In Orthographic mode, click and drag in any direction to scroll through the constellations. Note that if you scroll past a pole, left and right controls will be reversed. Right click to zoom back out to Plate Carré mode.

Plate Carré projection distorts the poles greatly, so to get a better idea of the polar constellations, zoom in to them.

Also, you may need to refresh the page a few times if there are visual glitches (missing stars or missing constellations). For some reason, the data doesn't always completely load.
