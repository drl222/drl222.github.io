Star Map

Use the buttons in the navigation pane to traverse the star map. Note that going beyond a pole will invert the left and right buttons. Find the constellation listed right below the navigation buttons, and click the constellation abbreviation in the star map. If HTML5 local storage is enabled, your progress will automatically be saved.

The data for the constellation borders is from http://www.iau.org/public/themes/constellations/, and the star data is from the Yale Bright Star Catalog http://tdc-www.harvard.edu/catalogs/bsc5.html
