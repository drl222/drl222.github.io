Sample Projects

Super Smash Bros. Character Usages

An interactive visualization of how often people play different characters in the video game Super Smash Bros. for 3DS and Wii U. The data was collected manually when I played online against randomly selected opponents. The visualization is made with D3.js (as well as HTML/CSS/JavaScript).

Star Map

An interactive map of the sky, presented as a game to find each of the eighty-eight constellations. The data about the stars and constellations was collected from the IAU website and the Yale Bright Star Catalog and organized in CSV format with Python. The map and game were created with the HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.

Projects in Adobe Flash and ActionScript

A few Flash files, ranging from games to educational tools, that I have made using Adobe Flash with ActionScript 2 and 3. The page itself is made with AngularJS.

MCD Art Explorer

A page that lets you search through Artstor gallery to look for pieces of art. I created the page itself using AngularJS and HTML, but the mechanics of the search were worked on by a research team at Drexel University. (Image: "Portrait of a Lady" from the School of Leonardo)
